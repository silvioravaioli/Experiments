Introduction

Welcome to the ECON 4850 experiment participation site. Please read these instructions carefully before participating.
The experiment will take approximately 1 hour of your time and consists of 3 sections. We encourage you to be in a quiet area without distractions while participating.
Please try your best to participate in each section consecutively. You may take a short break in between, but avoid long delays between sections.
After you are done with each section, a data file will automatically save to your computer. Please KEEP this file somewhere convenient.
Once you are ready, please click on the links below to start each experiment section. You are required to complete ALL THREE sections.
